    How Auto-Shortlisting Works

    Auto-shortlisting scans all Pending candidates and moves eligible ones to Shortlisted in one click, based on the criteria you set.

    Min Match %
    The minimum skill match score. Calculated by comparing the candidate's skills against the required skills for the role. 60%+ means most critical skills are present.
    Min Experience %
    The experience relevance score — how well the candidate's years of experience align with the seniority level of the position. 50%+ is a reasonable baseline.
    Position Filter
    Optional — only consider candidates who applied for a position containing this text. Leave blank to apply across all roles.
    What Happens After
    Moved candidates appear in Shortlisted. You can still review each one, move them back to Pending, or progress to Interviewing. Auto-shortlist never rejects — it only promotes.
    Tip: Start with Match % at 60% and adjust based on how many candidates you have and how competitive the role is.
